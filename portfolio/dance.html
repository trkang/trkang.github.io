    I've always thought dance was super cool, but was always too afraid to pursue it in fear of looking bad. In fall of 2019, I decided to step way out of my comfort zone and do something I never thought I would do, dance. And trust me, I was bad, like real bad. But this lead me to join a dance team called Level V Origins (LVO) that helped me progress along my dance journey. Fast forward like 6 months, I was definitely seeing major improvements from being a log to a somewhat flexible stick. It took way too many days and hours of practicing until or even past midnight, but it was definitely well worth it. Starting dance and joining LVO have been two of the most enjoyable and gratifying decisions I have made during college. If anything, I wish I had started dance sooner. Moral of the story, if you have always wanted to do something but was too afraid to step out of your comfort zone, just do it; you may find something that you really love. Anyways, here are some progression videos of me and my team performing in festivals, competitions, and showcases; I promise i'm in them acting like a log.
